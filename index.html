About

Bridging laboratory science and clinical diagnostics

I am a Research Officer at the Mycobacteriology Laboratory, Program for Emerging Infections, Infectious Diseases Division, icddr,b. My work centres on improving tuberculosis diagnostics — particularly for children and community settings — using AI-assisted chest X-ray reading and near-point-of-care molecular tests.

My earlier research covered enteric pathogens including Cryptosporidium, Entamoeba histolytica, and Giardia lamblia, and I continue to bring a multi-pathogen perspective to infectious disease research in Bangladesh.
